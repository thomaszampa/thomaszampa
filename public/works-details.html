Manage.Me

Relationship Management Software

“Manage.Me” is a web-based application designed to help creatives stay organized, driven, and stress free. Whether you are a performance artist, songwriter, designer, or anything in-between, “Manage.Me” can meet your specific needs.

By putting tools such as: goals, reminders, and growth tracking api’s in your hands, “Manage.Me” gives it's user the ability to customize their experience and utilize the tools they need to advance to the next level within their industry.

Taking this application to scale would include a refined UX/UI, continued integration of industry tools and APIs, and increased user custimazation to ensure every unique industry's needs can be met.

Stack
VueJS, Ruby on Rails, and Bootstrap

Dungeon Knight

2D Video Game

"Dungeon Knight" is a retro run-and-gun style 2D-platformer set to release summer 2019.

Reminiscent of classic 1990’s 16-bit games, "Dungeon Knight" transports you back in time to an era since forgotten. “Dungeon Knight” combines everything you love about this action-packed golden-age of gaming with the flawless physics of the mother of all platformer games, “Super Mario.”

“Dungeon Knight’s” unique design, custom C# physics, and intuitive UI make for an experience that will entertain any gamer.

Stack
C# and Unity
