Dr. Jekyll

Music Production and Performance

“Dr. Jekyll” is the beginning of a long-awaited creative project to bring Memphis “phonk,” lofi hip-hop, and other experimental sub-genres of beat music to the forefront of conversation.

As the name suggests, “Dr. Jekyll” shows off a different, more tame side of my musical endeavors. Pulling influence from jazz, hip-hop, and bossa-nova, this newest alias is an avenue to share not only my own production, but the production of those going unnoticed.

Expect to find “Dr. Jekyll” hidden in the back of your local speakeasy, art installation, or creative exposition curating sound, atmosphere, and environment.

Don Soldi

Music Production and Performance

Established in early 2015, “Don Soldi” began as a campaign to turn Nashville’s music scene on its head. In collaboration with close friend Nick Lara, a traditional turntablist from the greater Los Angeles area, “Don Soldi” brings together our roots in trap, soul, and electronic bass music to create a performance duo that is entirely unique.

By 2016 “Don Soldi” had evolved beyond unlicensed warehouse parties and floor bending house shows into a sound and professional live performance that felt eclectic, innovative, and unlike anything the city had seen or heard before.

Today “Don Soldi” can be found in the studio collaborating with cutting-edge artists, poolside supplying the party in hot Nashville summers, or at the silent disco flexing turntable and performance skills.

Ableton Live, Trackor, and Audio-Technica AT-LP120

PUNK

Sound Design and Composition

“PUNK” is a multi-medium depiction of identity developed over the course of several years by filmmaker and photographer Tris Bucaro. This film and metal print collection was on display February through March of 2017 at 721 Broadway, NY.

Creating the composition and sound design for “PUNK” allowed me to truly experiment with abstract soundscapes, atmosphere, and sense of space.

Mr. Bucaro and I have plans for several more multi-medium exhibitions in the coming years and beyond.

Film and Metal Print
